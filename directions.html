Getting to Zion

Zion is located at 3410 6th Ave, Tacoma, WA 98406

We're one block east of Union Avenue.

Coming from the south on I-5:

  • Take exit 132 to Washington 16
  • Take exit 1B off WA 16 to Union Ave
  • Turn right off exit and travel north to 6th Ave
  • Turn right onto 6th Ave and travel one block east to Zion

Coming from the north on I-5:

  • Take exit 132B to Washington 16
  • Take exit 1B off WA 16 to Union Ave
  • Turn right off exit and travel north to 6th Ave
  • Turn right onto 6th Ave and travel one block east to Zion
